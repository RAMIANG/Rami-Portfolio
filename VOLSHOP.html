OVERVIEW

As mobile usuage is on the rise,my team and I wanted to create a mobile version of the University of Tennessee's Store "Vol Shop"

Product Concept Statement: VolShop App As of right now there are no mobile applications in the market that serves a platform for anybody wanting to buy University of Tennessee at Knoxville gear. We are proposing a mobile application which would make it easy for anyone wanting to purchase UTK gear to promote Vol pride for faculty, alumni, students, and fans. Being able to buy gear/clothes off the app makes it easier to buy when not near a computer or pulling up the website on a mobile app.
